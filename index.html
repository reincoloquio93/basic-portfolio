About me

Hi, my name is Rein Coloquio. I live in Jersey City, New Jersey. I am currently enrolled in Rutgers Coding Bootcamp and slowly learning about HTML and CSS. Prior to pursuing this career, I work as a part time Ride-share driver and personal trainer. I didn’t want to continue on this path due to stress and fluctuation of my income. I didn’t see much room for growth and I did not have a rewarding feeling everyday for what I was doing. I had always enjoyed technology and computers but never knew any more than the average user. I want to become a web developer because I feel like I could make a living wage, in demand, create amazing work that people use everyday, and further challenge my intellectual.
